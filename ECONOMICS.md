# Unit Economics & Mathematical Feasibility

If Credex deploys the Audit Engine to production, here are the estimated metrics required to hit $1M in Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR) through this funnel alone within 18 months.

## Value of a Converted Lead
- Assuming a target Series A startup with 50 engineers.
- They optimize their stack with Credex moving to ~30 enterprise/team seats of Cursor/Claude equivalents. 
- Retail value: $30/seat * 30 seats * 12 months = $10,800/yr.
- If Credex secures these credits wholesale and sells at a margin resulting in $3,000/yr gross profit per converted logo.
- **Estimated Lead Value (if converted):** $3,000 ARR.

## Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)
- **Top of Funnel (Hacker News / X Threads):** Basically $0. Heavy time investment, but 0 dollar ad spend.
- **Server/Compute:** LLM Summaries (~$0.00025 per Anthropic Haiku call). Extremely cheap.
- Let's bake in a blended CAC of roughly **$50** (assuming time value of founders marketing it).

## The Conversion Funnel
1. **Audits Completed:** 20,000 companies test the public dashboard globally over a year.
2. **Report Gated Email Captures:** 10% (2,000 leads).
3. **Credex Consultations Booked (MQL):** Of those, we only target companies the engine flags with >$500/mo savings. Let's assume 15% of emails hit this criteria and book (300 Bookings).
4. **Close Rate (Deal Won):** 50% (because they are mathematically staring at free money through Credex). (150 closed deals).

**150 deals x $3,000 gross margin = $450,000 ARR secured in Year 1.**

## The Path to $1M ARR via this Funnel
To scale to $1M purely through this tool rather than enterprise outbound sales:
- We need exactly ~333 converted leads.
- At our funnel metrics above, this requires **44,000 total audits completed.**
- This justifies eventually running highly targeted B2B paid ads directly matching "Gitub Copilot pricing alternatives" to feed the exact top-of-funnel flow.
